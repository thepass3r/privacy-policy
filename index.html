Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-27

MiksCrossposting is a personal automation tool used to cross-post content created by its owner to their own social media accounts (Instagram, Threads, TikTok, Telegram).

This application does not collect, store, or share data from third-party users. It only accesses the account data necessary to publish content on behalf of the account owner via official platform APIs.

No personal data is sold or shared with third parties.
